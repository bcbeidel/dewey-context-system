## When to Use This Domain

**Load when**:
- Planning a research study (methodology selection)
- Executing research (following standards)
- Conducting systematic reviews (PRISMA 2020)
- Analyzing market or competitive landscape
- Studying user experience or usability
- Assessing organizational culture
- Synthesizing evidence from multiple sources

**Key standards**:
- PRISMA 2020 for systematic reviews
- Evidence-based methodology selection
- Quality assessment (AMSTAR-2, ROBIS)
- Rigorous data collection and analysis
